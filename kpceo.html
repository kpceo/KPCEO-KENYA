PASTORS AND CHRISTIAN EMPOWERMENT ORGANIZATION

By
kpceo

The Kenya pastors and Christians empowerment organization [KPCEO] was founded and initiated by wilson Wamusosi Makuma,Mr. Joseph Wasike Mapesa,Pastor Festus Muthembwa Katava and Mrs. Lydia Wanjiku Kamande by the year 2019 and the organization was registered in the year of 2020/21st/October..

The Kenya Pastors and Christians Empowerment Organization is the legal Organization that was registered in 2020 and is dedicated to empowering, imparting knowledge, skills, and awareness amongst the poorest vulnerable strata of the society with emphasis on supporting needy children, employment generation among youths and family couples and pastors, looking after poverty stricken, water harvesting, preventing health hazards, drug de-addiction, witness protection and awareness about environment and hygiene.

We offer ours services 24/7 to help us achieve our goal.

THE PROPOSED APPROACH

proposed approach decided mutually by implementing agency, community members and other stakeholders are:

i) Direct support of needy children, elderly and PLWDs with their needs i.e . education, food, clothing, medication etc.

ii) Construction of permanent facilities that is churches, education centers, vocational centers, children’s bible centers etc.

iii) Providing vocational skills to youths and family couples in entrepreneurship and trades with established market demand.

iv) Inculcation of social values in youths, PLWDs and family couples.

v) Supporting farmers with farm inputs that they may produce food on large scale for the communities.

vi) De stigmatizing PLWDs and other needy families through spiritual, social, economic and financial empowerment.

TARGET GROUP

Direct target group

The most vulnerable groups of people in the communities ie Needy children, youths, PLWDs, elderly persons, pastors, farmers and family couples. Their vulnerability will be judged by the extent of their relevance in the society as the interest shown by them towards the “Kenya Pastors and Christians Empowerment Organization” The Organization will select most vulnerable members from different communities as follows;

  • Needy children as well as orphans.
  • Persons Living With Disabilities.
  • Elderly persons.
  • Youths.
  • Pastors.
  • Farmers.
  • Women empowering
  • water scarce areas

    • Of all the above, the Organization expects at least 90% of the members to complete the project successfully.

    Intermediate Target group

    It will include Parents and guardians of needy children, orphans and youths, Religious leaders, Local Peoples Representatives, Local opinion leaders, Law enforcers, Churches, Teachers and retired teachers and Local communities.

    Conclusion

    KPCEO is the future of all the help.
