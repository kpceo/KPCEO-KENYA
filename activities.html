The Organization aims at implementing its programs through the following activities;

  1. Giving food, clothing, beddings, medical care to vulnerable needy children, people living with disabilities [PLWDs], elderly, pastors etc.
  2. Building permanent church structures and installing furniture and instruments and inspirational spiritual materials.
  3. offering educational support for needy children and youths school fees and tuition as well at their respective institutions so as to impart knowledge and skills to them in different communities.
  4. Giving farmers agriculture inputs i.e. good quality seeds, breeds of animals and birds, fertilizers, and sensitizing them on modern farming technology. And also creating awareness and motivation regarding environments i.e planting of trees, de-pollution and cleanness.
  5. Organizing entrepreneurship, life skills training and career guidance for youths, family couples, pastors and people living with disabilities[PLWDs] which will enable them get empowered economically.
  6. Giving counseling and guidance for youths, people living with disabilities[PLWDs] and family couples as well.
